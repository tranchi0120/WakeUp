ABOUT US

The Wake-up health monitoring application is primarily for office workers who spend extended periods of time sitting in front of computers or other electronic devices. When you turn on your computer, the program will start working and remind you to take a break if you've been working on it for too long. Wake-up offers basic exercises that you may undertake in the comfort of your own workspace. Besides, Wake-up is also a useful tool to help diagnose your health condition based on your symptoms and suggest a healthy eating plan for office workers.
